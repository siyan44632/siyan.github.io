Research Projects

Project 1 : Terahertz devices

(2016-2019)

(HUST, China     Supervisors: Furi Ling and Wengfen Yu)
  • Terahertz devices based on ferroelectric materials
    • 1) Investigated the interaction of THz wave and perovskite ferroelectric thin film, such as BaTiO3, PbTiO3 and so on;
    • 2) Investigated the lattice vibration in ferroelectrics under external fields, such as electric field and optical field probed by terahertz spectrometer;
    • 3) Investigated the interaction between THz wave and ferroelectric superlattice under external fields.
  • Terahertz devices based on meta-surfaces and 2D materials
    • 1) Designed and simulated the meta-surfaces by means of Numerical Software such as COMSOL and CST;
    • 2) Investigated the principle of the active tunable THz devices and explored its sensitivity to external fields

Project 2 : Super-resolution reconstruction for terahertz imaging

(2018-2019)

(HUST, China     Supervisors: Furi Ling)
  • Super-resolution reconstruction for terahertz imaging based on sub-pixel gradient field transform
    • 1) Assisted building the optical-pump-terahertz-probe (OPTP) system
    • 2) Improved the THz image quality based on the gradient-guided image super-resolution reconstruction
